# Team Profile: Cape Verde

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Bubista
- **Key Players**: Márcio Rosa, Vózinha, C.J. dos Santos, Steven Moreira, Logan Costa, Roberto Lopes, Diney Borges, Stopira, Sidny Lopes Cabral, Wagner Pina, Kelvin Pires, Deroy Duarte, Garry Rodrigues, Kevin Pina, Laros Duarte, Yannick Semedo, Telmo Arcanjo, Jamiro Monteiro, João Paulo, Willy Semedo, Ryan Mendes, Jovane Cabral, Gilson Tavares, Nuno Da Costa, Dailon Rocha Livramento, Hélio Varela
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Cape Verde implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Márcio Rosa to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Bubista demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Cape Verde excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

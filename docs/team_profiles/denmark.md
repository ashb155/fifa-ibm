# Team Profile: Denmark

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Kasper Hjulmand
- **Key Players**: Christian Eriksen, Pierre-Emile Højbjerg, Rasmus Højlund, Andreas Christensen
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-4-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Flexible system heavily reliant on overlapping center-backs and wing-back creativity.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Denmark implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-4-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Christian Eriksen to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Kasper Hjulmand demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Denmark excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-4-3 structure.

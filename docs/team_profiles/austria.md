# Team Profile: Austria

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Ralf Rangnick
- **Key Players**: David Alaba, Marcel Sabitzer, Konrad Laimer, Christoph Baumgartner
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-2-2-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Pure Red Bull school: aggressive counter-pressing and immediate verticality upon winning the ball.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Austria implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 4-2-2-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for David Alaba to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a aggressive high block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Ralf Rangnick demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Austria excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-2-2-2 structure.

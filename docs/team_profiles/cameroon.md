# Team Profile: Cameroon

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Marc Brys
- **Key Players**: André Onana, Vincent Aboubakar, Frank Anguissa, Bryan Mbeumo
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Physical and direct, often bypassing the midfield to find powerful forwards.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Cameroon implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for André Onana to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Marc Brys demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Cameroon excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

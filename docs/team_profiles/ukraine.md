# Team Profile: Ukraine
- **Manager**: Serhiy Rebrov
- **Key Player**: Oleksandr Zinchenko
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Defensive Inverting & Deep Buildup

## Tactical Breakdown
The Ukraine national team operates primarily under a 4-3-3 shape, focusing on a defensive inverting & deep buildup style of play. 

### Offensive Phase
During buildup, the manager Serhiy Rebrov instructs the team to look for Oleksandr Zinchenko, who serves as the key reference point in transitions. Wing progression and midfield rotations are crucial to breaking down opposition blocks.

### Defensive Phase
When out of possession, they drop into a compact shape to deny space in between the lines. Depending on their style, they either trigger an aggressive counter-press or establish a disciplined low block.

### Transitions
Quick transitions from defense to attack are prioritized to catch opponents disorganized, leveraging the pace and playmaking ability of Oleksandr Zinchenko.

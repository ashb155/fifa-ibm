# Team Profile: Costa Rica

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Gustavo Alfaro
- **Key Players**: Keylor Navas, Joel Campbell, Francisco Calvo, Manfred Ugalde
- **Preferred Formation**: 5-4-1
- **Tactical Identity**: Deep defensive block prioritizing clean sheets and counter-attacks.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Costa Rica implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 5-4-1 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Keylor Navas to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Gustavo Alfaro demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Costa Rica excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 5-4-1 structure.

# Team Profile: Serbia

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Dragan Stojković
- **Key Players**: Aleksandar Mitrović, Dušan Vlahović, Sergej Milinković-Savić, Dušan Tadić
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-4-1-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Extremely direct and physical, utilizing twin strikers and elite crossers of the ball.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Serbia implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 3-4-1-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Aleksandar Mitrović to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Dragan Stojković demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Serbia excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-4-1-2 structure.

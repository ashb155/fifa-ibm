# Team Profile: Uzbekistan

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Fabio Cannavaro
- **Key Players**: Abduvakhid Nematov, Botir Ergashev, Utkir Yusupov, Rustam Ashurmatov, Umar Eshmurodov, Abdukodir Khusanov, Jakhongir O'rozov, Farrukh Sayfiyev, Khodjiakbar Alijonov, Sherzod Nasrullayev, Abdulla Abdullayev, Bekhruz Karimov, Avazbek Ulmasaliev, Dostonbek Xamdamov, Otabek Shukurov, Oston Urunov, Jamshid Iskanderov, Akmal Mozgovoy, Abbosbek Fayzullaev, Azizjon Ganiev, Odiljon Hamrobekov, Sherzod Esanov, Eldor Shomurodov, Igor Sergeev, Ruslanbek Jiyanov, Azizbek Amonov
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Uzbekistan implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Abduvakhid Nematov to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Fabio Cannavaro demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Uzbekistan excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

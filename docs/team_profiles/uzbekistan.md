# Team Profile: Uzbekistan

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Srečko Katanec
- **Key Players**: Eldor Shomurodov, Jaloliddin Masharipov, Abbosbek Fayzullaev, Otabek Shukurov
- **Preferred Formation**: 5-3-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Defensive solidity combined with quick transitions led by their star forwards.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Uzbekistan implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 5-3-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Eldor Shomurodov to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Srečko Katanec demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Uzbekistan excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 5-3-2 structure.

# Team Profile: France

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Didier Deschamps
- **Key Players**: Kylian Mbappé, Antoine Griezmann, Aurélien Tchouaméni, William Saliba
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-2-3-1
- **Tactical Identity**: Pragmatic tournament football. Solid defensive base with lethal transition speeds.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
France implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-2-3-1 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Kylian Mbappé to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Didier Deschamps demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, France excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-2-3-1 structure.

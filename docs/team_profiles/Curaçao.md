# Team Profile: Curaçao

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Dick Advocaat
- **Key Players**: Trevor Doornbusch, Eloy Room, Tyrick Bodak, Roshon van Eijma, Joshua Brenet, Armando Obispo, Sheral Floranus, Riechedly Bazoer, Juriën Gaari, Shurandy Sambo, Ar'jany Martha, Livano Comenencia, Deveron Fonville, Tyrese Noslin, Leandro Bacuna, Godfried Roemeratoe, Juninho Bacuna, Kevin Felida, Gervane Kastaneer, Kenji Gorré, Jürgen Locadia, Tahith Chong, Sontje Hansen, Jearl Margaritha, Jeremy Antonisse, Brandley Kuwas
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Curaçao implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Trevor Doornbusch to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Dick Advocaat demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Curaçao excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Argentina

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Lionel Scaloni
- **Key Players**: Lionel Messi, Emiliano Martínez, Alexis Mac Allister, Enzo Fernández
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Fluid midfield rotation, relying on individual brilliance and high emotional intensity.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Argentina implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Lionel Messi to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Lionel Scaloni demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Argentina excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

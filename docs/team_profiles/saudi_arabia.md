# Team Profile: Saudi Arabia

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Roberto Mancini
- **Key Players**: Salem Al-Dawsari, Firas Al-Buraikan, Saud Abdulhamid, Ali Al-Bulaihi
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-5-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Extremely aggressive high defensive line with intense off-the-ball work rate.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Saudi Arabia implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-5-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Salem Al-Dawsari to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Roberto Mancini demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Saudi Arabia excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-5-2 structure.

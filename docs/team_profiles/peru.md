# Team Profile: Peru

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Jorge Fossati
- **Key Players**: Gianluca Lapadula, Renato Tapia, Pedro Gallese, André Carrillo
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-5-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Classic South American grit mixed with short-passing flair, utilizing wide wing-backs.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Peru implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-5-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Gianluca Lapadula to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Jorge Fossati demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Peru excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-5-2 structure.

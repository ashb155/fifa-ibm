# Team Profile: United States

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Mauricio Pochettino
- **Key Players**: Christian Pulisic, Weston McKennie, Tyler Adams, Folarin Balogun
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Athletic and direct, focusing on wing play and set pieces.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
United States implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Christian Pulisic to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Mauricio Pochettino demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, United States excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Jamaica

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Heimir Hallgrímsson
- **Key Players**: Leon Bailey, Michail Antonio, Ethan Pinnock, Demarai Gray
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-4-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Direct counter-attacking football relying on raw pace.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Jamaica implements a vertical and direct approach. Operating primarily in a 4-4-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Leon Bailey to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Heimir Hallgrímsson demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Jamaica excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-4-2 structure.

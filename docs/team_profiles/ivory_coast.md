# Team Profile: Ivory Coast

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Emerse Faé
- **Key Players**: Yahia Fofana, Alban Lafont, Mohamed Kone, Evan N'Dicka, Christopher Operi, Ghislain Konan, Wilfried Singo, Guela Doué, Emmanuel Agbadou, Odilon Kossounou, Ousmane Diomande, Franck Kessié, Seko Fofana, Ibrahim Sangaré, Jean Michaël Seri, Amad Diallo, Parfait Guiagon, Christ Ravynel Inao Oulaï, Nicolas Pépé, Evann Guessand, Ange-Yoan Bonny, Sepe Elye Wahi, Simon Adingra, Oumar Diakite, Bazoumana Touré, Yan Diomandé
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Ivory Coast implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Yahia Fofana to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Emerse Faé demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Ivory Coast excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Algeria

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Vladimir Petković
- **Key Players**: Luca Zidane, Oussama Benbout, Melvin Mastil, Aïssa Mandi, Rayan Aït Nouri, Ramy Bensebaini, Samir Chergui, Jaouen Hadjam, Rafik Belghali, Zinédine Belaïd, Amine Tougai, Achref Abada, Nabil Bentaleb, Houssem Aouar, Hicham Boudaoui, Ramiz Zerrouki, Fares Chaïbi, Ibrahim Maza, Yacine Titraoui, Adil Boulbina, Riyad Mahrez, Amine Gouiri, Mohammed Amoura, Nadhir Benbouali, Anis Moussa, Farés Ghedjemis
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Algeria implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Luca Zidane to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Vladimir Petković demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Algeria excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

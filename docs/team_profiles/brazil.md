# Team Profile: Brazil

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Dorival Júnior
- **Key Players**: Vinícius Júnior, Rodrygo, Bruno Guimarães, Alisson
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-2-3-1
- **Tactical Identity**: Samba flair mixed with European defensive solidity. Deadly in 1v1 situations on the wings.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Brazil implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-2-3-1 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Vinícius Júnior to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Dorival Júnior demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Brazil excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-2-3-1 structure.

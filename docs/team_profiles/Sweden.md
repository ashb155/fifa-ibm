# Team Profile: Sweden

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Jon Dahl Tomasson
- **Key Players**: Alexander Isak, Dejan Kulusevski, Viktor Gyökeres, Victor Lindelöf
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-4-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Modernized two-striker system emphasizing mobility and combination play up front.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Sweden implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-4-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Alexander Isak to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Jon Dahl Tomasson demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Sweden excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-4-2 structure.

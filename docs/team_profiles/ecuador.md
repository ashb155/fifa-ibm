# Team Profile: Ecuador

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Félix Sánchez
- **Key Players**: Moisés Caicedo, Piero Hincapié, Kendry Páez, Pervis Estupiñán
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-5-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Physical, high-energy pressing system utilizing dynamic wing-backs.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Ecuador implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-5-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Moisés Caicedo to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a aggressive high block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Félix Sánchez demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Ecuador excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-5-2 structure.

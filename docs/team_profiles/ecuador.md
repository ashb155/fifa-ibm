# Team Profile: Ecuador

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Sebastián Beccacece
- **Key Players**: Hernán Galíndez, Gonzalo Valle, Moisés Ramírez, Ángelo Preciado, Félix Torres, Pervis Estupiñán, William Pacho, Jackson Porozo, Piero Hincapié, Joel Ordoñez, Yaimar Medina, Alan Franco, Jordy Alcívar, Gonzalo Plata, Moisés Caicedo, Pedro Vite, Alan Minda, Ray Paez, Denil Castillo, Jordy Caicedo, Enner Valencia, John Yeboah, Anthony Valencia, Nilson Angulo, Kevin Rodríguez, Jeremy
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Ecuador implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Hernán Galíndez to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Sebastián Beccacece demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Ecuador excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

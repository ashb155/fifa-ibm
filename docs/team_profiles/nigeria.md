# Team Profile: Nigeria

## General Overview
- **Manager**: José Peseiro
- **Key Players**: Victor Osimhen, Ademola Lookman, Alex Iwobi, William Troost-Ekong
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-4-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Overflowing with attacking talent, using wing-backs to supply elite strikers.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Nigeria implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-4-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Victor Osimhen to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. José Peseiro demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Nigeria excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-4-3 structure.

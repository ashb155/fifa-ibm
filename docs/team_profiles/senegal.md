# Team Profile: Senegal

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Aliou Cissé
- **Key Players**: Sadio Mané, Kalidou Koulibaly, Pape Matar Sarr, Édouard Mendy
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Athletically superior, utilizing physical dominance in midfield and pace on the wings.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Senegal implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Sadio Mané to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Aliou Cissé demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Senegal excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

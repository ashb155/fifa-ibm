# Team Profile: Panama

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Thomas Christiansen
- **Key Players**: Adalberto Carrasquilla, Michael Amir Murillo, Ismael Díaz, Aníbal Godoy
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-4-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Fluid attacking shape with heavy emphasis on central overloads.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Panama implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-4-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Adalberto Carrasquilla to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Thomas Christiansen demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Panama excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-4-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Türkiye

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Head Coach of Türkiye
- **Key Players**: Star Player of Türkiye, Key Midfielder for Türkiye
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-2-3-1
- **Tactical Identity**: Compact block and counter-attack

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
The team prefers to build from the back. Under the 4-2-3-1 formation, the fullbacks provide width while the central midfielders dictate the tempo. When facing high pressure, they can transition quickly.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, Türkiye maintains a structured shape. They press aggressively in the middle third to force turnovers. Against stronger opposition, they drop into a mid-block to limit space behind the defensive line.

### Transitions
In offensive transitions, Türkiye looks to exploit wide areas immediately. Defensively, they prefer counter-pressing to win the ball back high up the pitch before falling back into their established shape.

# Team Profile: Turkey

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Vincenzo Montella
- **Key Players**: Mert Günok, Altay Bayındır, Uğurcan Çakır, Çağlar Söyüncü, Kaan Ayhan, Ferdi Kadıoğlu, Mert Müldür, Zeki Çelik, Abdülkerim Bardakcı, Ozan Kabak, Merih Demiral, Samet Akaydın, Evren Eren Elmalı, Salih Özcan, Hakan Çalhanoğlu, Orkun Kökçü, İsmail Yüksek, Arda Guler, Oguz Aydin, Baris Yilmaz, İrfan Kahveci, Yunus Akgün, Muhammed Kerem Aktürkoğlu, Deniz Gul, Kenan Yıldız, Can Uzun
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Turkey implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Mert Günok to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Vincenzo Montella demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Turkey excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Morocco

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Walid Regragui
- **Key Players**: Achraf Hakimi, Brahim Díaz, Hakim Ziyech, Yassine Bounou
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Impenetrable mid-block transitioning into lightning-fast wide attacks.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Morocco implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Achraf Hakimi to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Walid Regragui demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Morocco excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Morocco

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Mohamed Ouahbi
- **Key Players**: Munir Mohamedi, Yassine Bounou, Ahmed Reda Tagnaouti, Achraf Hakimi, Noussair Mazraoui, Issa Diop, Nayef Aguerd, Anass Salah-Eddine, Chadi Riad, Redouane Halhal, Zakaria El Ouahdi, Youssef Belammari, Sofyan Amrabat, Brahim Diaz, Azzedine Ounahi, Ismael Saibari, Neil El Aynaoui, Bilal El Khannouss, Chemsdine Talbi, Ayyoub Bouaddi, Samir El Mourabet, Soufiane Rahimi, Ayoub El Kaabi, Abdessamad Ezzalzouli, Gessime Yassine, Ayoube Amaimouni-Echghouyab
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Morocco implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Munir Mohamedi to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Mohamed Ouahbi demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Morocco excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

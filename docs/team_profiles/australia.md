# Team Profile: Australia

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Tony Popović
- **Key Players**: Maty Ryan, Paul Izzo, Patrick Beach, Aziz Behich, Miloš Degenek, Kai Trewin, Jacob Italiano, Cameron Burgess, Harry Souttar, Jason Geria, Jordan Bos, Alessandro Circati, Lucas Herrington, Mathew Leckie, Jackson Irvine, Connor Metcalfe, Ajdin Hrustic, Aiden O'Neill, Awer Mabil, Cameron Devlin, Paul Okon-Engstler, Mohamed Toure, Nishan Velupillay, Cristian Volpato, Nestor Irankunda, Tete Yengi
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Australia implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Maty Ryan to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Tony Popović demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Australia excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

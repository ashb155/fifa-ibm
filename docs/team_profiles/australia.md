# Team Profile: Australia

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Tony Popovic
- **Key Players**: Mathew Ryan, Jackson Irvine, Harry Souttar, Craig Goodwin
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-4-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Rugged, physically dominant, exceptional on set pieces due to towering center-backs.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Australia implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-4-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Mathew Ryan to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Tony Popovic demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Australia excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-4-2 structure.

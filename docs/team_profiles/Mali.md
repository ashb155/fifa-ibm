# Team Profile: Mali

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Éric Chelle
- **Key Players**: Yves Bissouma, Amadou Haidara, Kamory Doumbia, Hamari Traoré
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Fierce midfield pressing, looking to win the ball high and strike quickly.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Mali implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Yves Bissouma to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a aggressive high block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Éric Chelle demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Mali excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

# Team Profile: Paraguay

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Gustavo Alfaro
- **Key Players**: Gastón Olveira, Roberto Fernández, Orlando Gill, Fabián Balbuena, Gustavo Gómez, Júnior Alonso, Omar Alderete, Juan Cáceres, Gustavo Velázquez, José Canale, Alexandro Maidana, Andrés Cubas, Kaku, Miguel Almirón, Mauricio, Braian Ojeda, Matías Galarza, Damian Bobadilla, Diego Gómez, Antonio Sanabria, Gabriel Ávalos, Isidro Pitta, Ramón Sosa, Julio Enciso, Gustavo Caballero, Alex Arce
- **Preferred Formation**: 4-3-3
- **Tactical Identity**: Balanced tactical approach.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Paraguay implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 4-3-3 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Gastón Olveira to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Gustavo Alfaro demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Paraguay excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 4-3-3 structure.

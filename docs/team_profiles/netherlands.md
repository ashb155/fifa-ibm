# Team Profile: Netherlands

## General Overview
- **Manager**: Ronald Koeman
- **Key Players**: Virgil van Dijk, Frenkie de Jong, Cody Gakpo, Xavi Simons
- **Preferred Formation**: 3-4-1-2
- **Tactical Identity**: Total football principles, building from a back three and utilizing flying wingbacks.

## Tactical Breakdown
### Attacking Phase
Netherlands implements a patient build-up and positional play approach. Operating primarily in a 3-4-1-2 shape, the midfield looks to establish dominance early. When progressing the ball, they look for Virgil van Dijk to act as the primary catalyst.

### Defensive Structure
Defensively, they utilize a disciplined mid/low block. The defensive line coordinates closely with the midfield pivot to deny space between the lines. Ronald Koeman demands extreme discipline out of possession.

### Transitions
In transition moments, Netherlands excels at immediate reactions. Offensively, they target the half-spaces instantly upon regaining the ball. Defensively, they attempt to cut off passing lanes and delay the opposition to reform their 3-4-1-2 structure.

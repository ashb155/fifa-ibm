# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Football fans, analysts, and tacticians who want to analyze matches deeply using data-driven insights. They read match timelines and query the tactical RAG system, often in low-light environments (on the couch or at a bar watching a match). They value speed, clarity, and explainable insights over flashy graphics.

## Product Purpose
Stratos is a tactical-explainability companion for football matches. It connects fans directly to StatsBomb game timelines and Granite-backed RAG query completions to understand *why* tactical events occurred, proving its reasoning with source citations and broadcast-grade visualization.

## Brand Personality
Analytical, precise, authoritative, broadcast-grade.
3-word personality: Broadcast, Clinical, Explainable.
Emotional goals: Evoke the feeling of sitting in a professional analysis studio or looking at high-end broadcast telemetry.

## Anti-references
- Consumer sports apps overflowing with betting odds, bright red/green indicator badges, and cluttered tables.
- Typical warm-cream SaaS templates (cream bg, rounded cards, cute emojis).
- Decorative glassmorphism or neon purple gradients.

## Design Principles
1. **Explainability Built Into Layout**: Every answer must have a citation tag (where it came from), explanation rail (the reasoning), and live telemetry (the data match context).
2. **Borrowed Light**: System chrome is desaturated and neutral cyan-on-navy. Team personalization color only ignites as dynamic "borrowed light" (deliberate glows or borders) rather than re-skinning the entire layout.
3. **Tactical Color Restraint**: Colors like yellow and red are reserved strictly for actual disciplinary cards or extreme timeline moments, never for generic UI state warnings or decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target: WCAG 2.2 AA.
- High-contrast text readability (cool white on stadium pitch night backgrounds).
- Respect user preference for reduced motion (`@media (prefers-reduced-motion)` support).
